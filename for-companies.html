Common questions

Do you guarantee a LinkedIn post?

No. We connect you with genuine advocates — people who actually use and like your product. Whether they post, and how, is between you and them. What we guarantee is the introduction to a real, vetted advocate (or money back in 30 days).

What if our product isn't in your database yet?

We'll source advocates. Send us your product URL and target audience profile. We'll find 2-3 genuine fans (real users on LinkedIn) and bring the best match to you. Still $25 — same guarantee.

Do advocates know they're being introduced to a company?

Yes. Every advocate on StackBoost signed up knowing they might be connected with companies. They opted in to be matched. No surprise cold pitches.

How is compensation structured?

That's between you and the advocate. Common arrangements: gift cards, product credits, cash payment, or just appreciation. Our platform introduction doesn't require or mandate any particular arrangement.

What verticals do you focus on?

B2B SaaS, with depth in MarTech, Sales tools, CRM, and CS platforms. If your product is in a different B2B vertical, reach out — we expand based on demand.
