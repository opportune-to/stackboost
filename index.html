Common questions

Do I have to write something positive?

Write what's true. Companies want authentic posts from real users, not fake hype. If you genuinely love a tool, say what you love about it and why. Authentic posts perform better anyway.

What if I don't like the company's offer?

You're never obligated to post. We'll reach out when there's an opportunity — you decide whether to accept. No commitment on signup, no pressure.

How is this different from influencer deals?

Influencers are hired to promote products they may never use. StackBoost only matches companies with people who already use and like their product. Audiences can tell the difference.

What's the catch?

None for advocates. We charge companies to connect with you, not the other way around. Early advocates get priority placement when companies start looking.
